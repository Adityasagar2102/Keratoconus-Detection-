Introduction

Welcome to our Keratoconus Detection System, a groundbreaking digital platform designed to revolutionize the early detection and monitoring of Keratoconus. Keratoconus is a progressive eye disorder that leads to the thinning and bulging of the cornea, causing vision distortion and sensitivity to light. Early detection is crucial for managing this condition effectively and preventing severe visual impairment.
